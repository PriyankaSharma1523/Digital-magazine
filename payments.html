Digital Payments

Digital payments allow money transfers without using physical cash. They have become a cornerstone of modern commerce because they offer:

  • Speed — instant transactions via UPI, wallets, or cards.
  • Security — encrypted payment gateways and OTP verification.
  • Convenience — pay using smartphones, smartwatches, or online portals.
  • Transparency — clear transaction records for both buyers and sellers.

Popular methods include UPI (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm), credit/debit cards, and net banking. Digital payments are enabling a cashless economy.
