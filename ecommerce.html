E-Commerce

E-commerce refers to buying and selling goods and services online. It has transformed retail by providing:

  • 24/7 shopping convenience from anywhere in the world.
  • Global market reach for sellers and service providers.
  • Personalized recommendations through AI and data analytics.
  • Lower operational costs compared to physical stores.

Examples include Amazon, Flipkart, Myntra, and Shopify-based stores. E-commerce continues to grow rapidly, fueled by faster delivery systems and improved online security.
