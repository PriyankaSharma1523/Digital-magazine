Changing Consumer Behaviour

The digital era has changed how people shop, decide, and engage with brands. Key trends include:

  • Preference for online shopping over physical stores.
  • Demand for personalized recommendations and offers.
  • Use of social media for product research and reviews.
  • Growing awareness of sustainability and ethical sourcing.

Consumers now expect fast delivery, easy returns, and secure transactions. Businesses that adapt to these expectations gain customer loyalty and long-term growth.
